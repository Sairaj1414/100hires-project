# Executive Summary

This research analyzes Simon Høiberg's LinkedIn content to understand how modern SaaS businesses are being built using AI agents, automation, self-hosting, and extremely lean teams.

Unlike traditional startup advice, Simon advocates using AI to replace repetitive work, allowing solo founders and small teams to build million-dollar SaaS companies.

---

# Core Philosophy

Build software that creates freedom.

Use AI to automate operations.

Own your infrastructure.

Keep teams lean.

Optimize for independence instead of headcount.

- AI should automate support, development, marketing and operations
- Humans provide strategy and judgment

## 7. AI Security

Recurring advice:

- Restrict permissions
- Separate reasoning from execution
