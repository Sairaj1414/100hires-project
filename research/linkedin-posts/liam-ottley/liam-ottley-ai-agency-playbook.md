**Date Reviewed:** 25 June 2026

---

# Executive Summary

This research analyzes Liam Ottley's LinkedIn content to understand how modern AI agencies are built, marketed, productized, and scaled.

His content consistently focuses on helping businesses become AI-first through AI Operating Systems (AIOS), AI agents, consulting, education, SaaS, and enterprise automation.

---

# Core Philosophy

AI is no longer just about chatbots.

Businesses need an operating system where AI understands company data, remembers context, automates workflows, and continuously improves.

The winners will be companies that implement AI across every department rather than deploying isolated automations.

# Business Lessons

Repeated advice:

- Productize services.
- Build repeatable systems.
- Charge retainers.
- Focus on ROI.
- Build an audience first.
- Sell outcomes instead of technology.
- Keep improving client systems over time.

3. Framework or checklist

# Key Takeaways

- AI Operating Systems are a long-term competitive advantage.
- Consulting creates better AI implementations than jumping straight into development.
- Voice AI is a rapidly growing service category.
- Persistent memory dramatically improves AI agents.
- Agencies should focus on transformation, not isolated tools.
- Content builds trust and generates inbound leads.
- Validate demand with services before creating SaaS.
