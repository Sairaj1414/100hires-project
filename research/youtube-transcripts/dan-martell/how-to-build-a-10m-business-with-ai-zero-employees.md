# How to Build a $10M Business with AI (Zero Employees)

**Expert:** Dan Martell  
**Source:** YouTube  
**Date Collected:** 24/06/2026  
**Topic:** AI Automation, SaaS Scaling, Business Systems, Founder Productivity

## Summary

Dan Martell explains how founders can use AI to remove operational bottlenecks and scale a business without continuously adding employees. Rather than treating AI as a simple assistant, he demonstrates how it can automate sales, operations, customer delivery, and documentation, allowing founders to focus on strategic work.

## Key Takeaways

### 1. Identify the Bottleneck (1:13–3:15)
- Analyze the entire business workflow:
  - Attention
  - Leads
  - Sales
  - Delivery
  - Retention
- Fix the stage where growth is constrained before buying new AI tools.

### 2. Automate Sales & Delivery (3:34–6:30)
AI can:
- Find qualified prospects
- Filter low-quality leads
- Personalize outreach
- Automate customer onboarding
- Increase delivery capacity without hiring

### 3. The 10-80-10 Rule (8:02–9:24)
For creative work:
- First 10% → Human strategy and direction
- Middle 80% → AI execution
- Final 10% → Human review and refinement

This keeps quality high while maximizing speed.

### 4. Replace Yourself Using the Camcorder Method (10:56–12:02)
- Record yourself completing repetitive tasks.
- Transcribe the workflow.
- Feed the process into AI.
- Allow AI to perform repeatable work consistently.

### 5. Build Systems, Not Dependence
The objective is to make yourself less involved in day-to-day operations every quarter by documenting processes and delegating execution to AI.
