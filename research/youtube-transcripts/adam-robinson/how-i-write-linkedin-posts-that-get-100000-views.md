# How I Write LinkedIn Posts That Get 100,000+ Views

**Expert:** Adam Robinson  
**Source:** YouTube  
**Topic:** LinkedIn Content Strategy, Personal Branding, B2B SaaS Marketing

## Summary

In this course, Adam Robinson explains his six-step framework for building a personal brand on LinkedIn that consistently generates business opportunities. He demonstrates how authentic content, optimized profiles, consistent engagement, and community building help founders create trust, attract inbound leads, and grow B2B SaaS companies without relying on paid advertising.

## Key Themes

### 1. Develop Your Story (0:34 - 4:30)

- Be authentic and transparent.
- Share personal experiences and opinions.
- Build a recognizable niche.
- Don't be afraid to have strong viewpoints.

### 2. Perfect Your Profile (14:38 - 18:24)

- Treat your profile as a landing page.
- Optimize your profile photo, banner, headline, and About section.
- Include clear calls to action and featured links.

### 3. Systematic Content Creation (18:24 - 27:20)

- Write for people, not algorithms.
- Maintain a swipe file for inspiration.
- Use repeatable content templates.
- Begin every post with a compelling hook.

### 4. Growth Tactics (27:20 - 29:58)

- Send targeted connection requests daily.
- Build relationships before selling.
- Avoid immediate sales pitches.
- Focus on long-term trust.

### 5. Build Community (29:58 - 33:36)

- Comment consistently on industry posts.
- Engage in conversations.
- Learn directly from audience feedback.
- Build relationships through discussions.

### 6. Leverage Video (33:36 - 36:45)

- Use video to strengthen personal branding.
- Show personality and expertise.
- Build stronger human connections.
- Increase credibility with visual content.

## Key Takeaways for B2B SaaS YouTube Strategy

1. Founder-led content builds trust faster than company content.
2. Strong storytelling increases audience engagement.
3. Consistent educational content creates long-term inbound demand.
4. Community engagement is as important as publishing content.
5. Video content strengthens personal branding across multiple platforms.
6. Repurpose LinkedIn content into YouTube videos and vice versa.

## Research Notes

This video is highly relevant because it provides a practical framework for founder-led content marketing. Although focused on LinkedIn, the principles of storytelling, authenticity, community engagement, and educational content directly apply to building a successful YouTube content strategy for B2B SaaS companies.
