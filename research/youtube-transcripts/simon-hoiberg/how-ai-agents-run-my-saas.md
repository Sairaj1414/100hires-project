# How AI Agents Run My SaaS

**Expert:** Simon Høiberg  
**Source:** YouTube  
**Topic:** AI Agents, SaaS Operations, Content Automation, and Business Systems

## Summary

In this video, Simon Høiberg demonstrates how he uses AI agents powered by OpenClaw and Hermes to automate major parts of his SaaS business. Rather than using AI for simple tasks, he employs specialized agents to manage coding, infrastructure monitoring, customer support, content creation, and advertising workflows.

## Key Themes

### Coding with AI Agents (0:37)

- AI agents manage repositories and development servers.
- Simon communicates through voice notes rather than writing code directly.
- The system enables rapid software development while maintaining quality control.
- AI-assisted development significantly increases output and efficiency.

### System Monitoring (2:24)

- AI agents monitor infrastructure around the clock.
- Agents have access to Grafana dashboards and server logs.
- Issues are investigated automatically before human intervention is required.
- Escalation occurs only when necessary.

### Customer Support Automation (3:51)

- AI integrates with support systems and databases.
- Customer issues are analyzed using historical context and system data.
- Support workflows become more efficient and accurate.
- Less manual switching between tools is required.

### Content Creation Workflows (5:35)

- Long-form voice notes are converted into structured content.
- AI generates social media posts and supporting assets.
- Graphics are created automatically using tools like Remotion.
- Content is scheduled directly into publishing platforms.

### AI-Powered Advertising Analysis (7:16)

- AI combines data from Meta Ads, Google Search, and Stripe.
- Low-quality traffic and ineffective keywords are identified automatically.
- AI provides objective analysis without emotional bias.
- Campaign optimization becomes more data-driven.

## Key Takeaways for B2B SaaS YouTube Strategy

1. AI can significantly accelerate content production workflows.
2. Founder ideas can be transformed into publishable content through automation.
3. Consistent content creation becomes easier with AI systems.
4. Workflow automation allows founders to focus on strategy.
5. Data-driven decision making improves marketing performance.

## Research Notes

This video is highly relevant because it demonstrates how SaaS founders can use AI to scale content production, marketing operations, and business processes. The content creation workflow is especially valuable for understanding how modern B2B SaaS companies can maintain consistent publishing without large teams.
