# YouTube Content Strategy for B2B SaaS

## Project Overview

This repository contains research on how successful B2B SaaS founders, entrepreneurs, marketers, and creators use YouTube and content marketing to grow software businesses.

The objective is to identify common frameworks, content strategies, Go-To-Market (GTM) approaches, founder branding techniques, and customer acquisition methods that can be applied when building a B2B SaaS YouTube channel.

---

# Research Objective

The primary goal of this research is to understand:

* How successful SaaS founders build an audience.
* How educational content generates customers.
* Founder-led marketing strategies.
* AI-powered content production.
* Product positioning and messaging.
* Go-To-Market (GTM) frameworks.
* Product-Market Fit (PMF).
* Customer acquisition strategies.
* LinkedIn content systems.
* AI automation for marketing and operations.

# Why This Research Matters

Modern B2B SaaS companies increasingly rely on founder-led content instead of traditional advertising.

The experts researched consistently demonstrate that educational YouTube videos, LinkedIn content, community building, and AI-powered workflows can create sustainable customer acquisition channels while strengthening brand authority.

# Outcome

This repository serves as a structured knowledge base that can later be used to create a comprehensive **B2B SaaS YouTube Content Strategy Playbook**, combining insights from experienced founders, operators, marketers, and creators into a practical framework for future implementation.
